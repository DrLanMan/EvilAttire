About EvilAttire

Welcome to EvilAttire, where darkness meets fashion in the most captivating way. Founded by Landon Martin and Steven Le, two college students with a shared passion for embracing the allure of darker aesthetics, EvilAttire is more than just a clothing brand – it's an expression of our admiration for the charismatic villains in popular culture.

As the photographer and designer behind EvilAttire, I, Landon, find inspiration in the shadows, capturing the essence of our clothing line through striking imagery that embodies the enigmatic and alluring vibe of the urban, darker side of fashion. Meanwhile, Steven, not only a model but also a budding photographer, lends his distinctive flair to our creative endeavors, bringing a unique perspective to our designs.

EvilAttire isn't about promoting malevolence or endorsing darkness in any harmful sense. Instead, our vision is to embrace the spirit of characters from movies, shows, and musicians who have skillfully portrayed the charisma of villains, influencing generations with their iconic style. Our fashion lines are a tribute to their powerful presence and an exploration of how darkness can be combined with fashion to create a captivating and distinct aesthetic.

At EvilAttire, we believe that fashion is a form of art and self-expression. Our clothing collections are thoughtfully curated to empower individuals who share our passion for the darker side of fashion, embracing their own uniqueness with confidence and style. Each piece in our lineup is designed to make you feel extraordinary and emboldened, embracing your inner anti-hero and celebrating the duality of light and dark within us all.

We invite you to join us on this journey of self-expression, where our fashion lines embody the spirit of enigmatic villains, allowing you to embrace your individuality with an edge of rebellious charm. Whether you resonate with the world of emo, gothic, or simply admire the allure of the dark side, EvilAttire is here to accompany you on your quest to look good, feel good, and exude an unmistakable aura of confident elegance.

Thank you for being a part of our ever-evolving narrative. Together, let's redefine fashion through the lens of darkness and embark on a style adventure like no other.


Stay bold, stay stylish,

Landon Martin & Steven Le
